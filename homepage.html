New Drivers Simulator

Purpose and Mission Goal

For new drivers it could be difficult to practice parking on the road, so we created this website to help visualize parking for them. About 16% of adults in America do not have a driver's license. While there is a myriad of reasons as to why that is, one reason is that people are timid and are afraid of learning to drive because it exposes them to real cars and they are worried about causing accidents. Aside from adults, there is a large portion of New drivers that are under the age of 18. It is also imperative for these teens to learn how to drive and the more resources there are the better. This website simulates cars in a parking lot driving and also parking via css animations.
